Chicken and rice

My Chicken and rice dish (one i made earlier)

This is a really nice dish and is not a rip off of nandos chicken and rice in any way shape or form

Ingredients

  • Microwave rice 1 bag
  • Chicken breasts 2
  • James' Special Sauce
  • Lemonade

Steps

  1. Turn the oven on Fan 200 and shove your breasts in for 30 mins
  2. Do some Odin Project lessons while you wait
  3. Tear the top of the bag 2cm and shove the rice in the microwave
  4. Dont burn yourself
  5. Use your common sense
