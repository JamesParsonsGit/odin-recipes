Tuna Pasta Bake

My Tuna pasta bake dish (one i made earlier)

This is a really nice dish and you should try it, really quick and easy

Ingredients

  • Pasta 75g (uncooked)
  • Tuna 50g
  • Cheese
  • Milk 200ml

Steps

  1. Weigh the pasta
  2. Boil the kettle
  3. Pour the water into the pan
  4. Add the pasta and let it boil for 10 mins
  5. Use your common sense
